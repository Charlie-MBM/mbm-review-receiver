Acquisition Funnel

All-source conversion funnel (GSC + Google Ads + GA4 + Hint): impressions → site visits → inquiries → consults → new members. Window: the conversion stages (visits → new members) are month-to-date. The top impressions band is search reach only (organic GSC + paid Google Ads), carries no impression→visit rate (direct/referral/social visits have no upstream impression), and is the one mixed-window element here — its paid half is actual month-to-date, and its organic half is an estimate — because GSC finalizes 2–3 days late, the current month's organic is prorated (last complete month's impressions ÷ its days × days elapsed) and flagged "est." Visits & inquiries are split by traffic source; booked new-member consults and new members come from Hint (no web channel — consults are a count, members split by plan type, Concierge + SO).

New members by lead source — self-reported in Hint (how each member first reached the practice; may predate the membership for existing patients). This is the relationship/offline attribution that GA4 & paid tracking can't see.

Website & SEOmanual

Auto-updating from Google Search Console, Rank Tracker & Site Explorer via your Ahrefs project.

⏱
Reporting-lag note: Google Search Console finalizes data 2–3 days after the fact, so the current month is always partial until it settles — and just after a reconnect it can take a few days to fully backfill. Treat recent dips in impressions, clicks, CTR, average position and the funnel’s top two stages as incomplete data, not real drops — they firm up over the following days.

Windows vary by metric (SEO data spans different ranges by nature): KPI cards = latest complete month · clicks/impressions & position charts = last 12 months · keyword "GSC 6mo" column = trailing 6 months · Domain Rating & referring domains = current.
